Instructions

All aboard the Blitzr Bus! In this game, each player is given one card at a time with a question associated with each card. If the questions are answered correctly, they can give a pre-specified amount of drinks to other players. If answered incorrectly, they must take that same number of drinks themselves!

After all players receive 4 cards, the next 8 cards in the deck are placed face down. One at a time, each one is flipped, and if a player has that card, they can discard it from their hand and give or take drinks.

After all 8 cards are flipped, the players with the most cards remaining in their hand must RIDE THE BUS! To get off the bus, the player(s) must successfully answer 4 consecutive questions!
